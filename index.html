What we do

Our service makes an automated call to cellular or landline numbers of your loved oves (all over the world*) and asks them to enter a secret PIN that you have provided to them in advance. 

All they need to do is pick up the call and enter the 4 digit pin indicating they are safe. We do multiple retries (based on your chosen plan) and inform you via Text Message once they verify themselves with the PIN.

Standard Wireless Charges Apply
*Coutries we can call to verify : US, UK, Most of Europe, New Zeleand, Asutralia, Mexico, India
